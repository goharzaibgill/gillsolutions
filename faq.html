Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does a typical project take?
Large-scale enterprise projects typically take 8-12 weeks, while high-performance landing pages can be delivered in as little as 3 weeks. Our agile methodology ensures rapid deployment without compromising structural integrity.
Do you offer post-launch maintenance?
Yes, we provide "White-Glove" maintenance packages that include 24/7 technical guard, security vault updates, and constant speed optimization to keep your product ahead of the curve.
What technologies do you specialize in?
We engineer solutions using cutting-edge stacks: React & Next.js for high-speed frontends, Node.js & Python for scalable backends, and Flutter for cross-platform mobile dominance.
Can you help with UI/UX design only?
Absolutely. Our elite design lab specializes in motion systems, strategic wireframing, and high-fidelity prototypes that define industry standards.
How do you handle project payments?
We operate on a performance-based milestone schedule: 30% Strategy Phase, 40% Development Sprint, and 30% Final Global Deployment. Transparency is our core value.
